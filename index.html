Frequently Asked Questions

Is this safe to use?

Yes! We only send a small amount (0.0002 ETH) to our test address. The transaction is fully controlled by you and requires your initial confirmation.

What information do you collect?

We only access your public wallet address to verify connection. No private keys or sensitive information is ever requested or stored.

Why perform a real transaction?

A real transaction helps verify that your wallet is properly configured and can successfully send funds, which is important for security testing.
